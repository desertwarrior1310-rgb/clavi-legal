Privacy Policy & Disclaimer

Last updated: June 2026

Your privacy is central to Clavi. This page explains, in plain language, what the app does and does not do with your information, and the limits of what the app provides.

A. What we collect

We designed Clavi to work without collecting personal information. To use the core features you do not need to provide your name, email address, phone number, or any account. We do not require you to sign in. Clavi does not require location, microphone, or camera permissions for normal use.

B. Where your data lives

Your decisions, answers, confidence levels, outcome tags, private notes, mood logs, Within task list, and related practice history are stored only on your device, in local storage. This information is not uploaded to us, is not sent to our servers, and is not shared with any third party by the app. Most features work fully offline.

If you uninstall the app or clear its data, this information is removed from your device and cannot be recovered by us, because we never had a copy.

C. Encryption and device security

The app uses device security features to protect local data. Premium backup files you create are encrypted on your device before you save or share them. The security of your data also depends on your device: keep your operating system updated and protect your device with a screen lock. Anyone with access to an unlocked device may be able to open the app.

D. Saved items and preferences

When you save an insight or product, or set preferences such as theme, those choices are stored locally on your device only.

E. External links and developer promotions

The app may show product cards and insights that contain links to external websites or stores. When you tap such a link, you leave the app and are subject to the privacy practices and terms of that third party.

The developer may publish genuine promotional content — for example on the Flash Screen or in the Store. This is developer-selected material, not third-party ad networks or behavioural tracking.

F. Subscriptions and payments

If you purchase Premium, payment is processed by Google Play (or the platform app store), not by us. The store handles your payment details according to its own policies. We do not receive or store your card or banking information.

G. Children

Clavi is intended for adults aged 18 and over and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

H. Analytics and tracking

The app does not embed third-party advertising or tracking SDKs intended to profile you. Developer-published promotions are not behavioural ad networks.

I. Disclaimer of advice

Clavi is a reflection tool only. It does not provide medical, psychological, legal, financial, investment, tax, career, relationship, or safety advice, and must not be used as a substitute for consultation with a qualified professional.

J. No guarantees; your responsibility

We make no promises about outcomes. You remain solely responsible for your decisions and their consequences. To the fullest extent permitted by law, the developer is not liable for any loss or damage arising from your use of the app or its content.

K. Crisis resources

The app cannot help in an emergency. If you are in danger or in crisis, contact your local emergency number or a qualified crisis line immediately.

L. Changes to this notice

We may update this notice as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected on this page. Your continued use of the app indicates acceptance.

M. Data requests

Because your content stays on your device and we do not collect it, we are unable to access, export, or delete it on your behalf. You control it directly on your device (including Premium backup and restore).

By using Clavi you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
